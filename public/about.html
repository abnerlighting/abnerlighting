About Us

Founded in 2015, Lightworx—the force behind the Abner brand—specializes in high-impact, precision-engineered lighting solutions. We blend cutting-edge technology with timeless aesthetics to illuminate spaces with intention and artistry.

At Abner, we believe lighting is more than function—it's emotion, mood, and experience. Every project we touch reflects our commitment to sustainability, craftsmanship, and performance. From retail showrooms to luxury residences, we design lighting that transforms architecture into atmosphere.

Join us in redefining the future of lighting—where every beam is deliberate, and every detail matters.

GLOBAL VISION. LOCAL CRAFT.

Our Mission

Rooted in Indian expertise and inspired by European design, Abner stands at the crossroads of global sensibility and local understanding. Our mission: to deliver performance-driven, beautiful lighting tailored to Indian lifestyles and climates.

Whether it's a bespoke residential chandelier or high-precision commercial lighting, our studio and manufacturing units deliver with exactitude and agility.

From concept to execution, we create lighting solutions that speak the language of design, innovation, and refined craftsmanship.

CHRONOSCAPE

A brief history of Abner

2015

Abner launches. Office and assembly line begin operations.

2016

Play Series and high-CRI LEDs introduced for retail lighting.

2017

Debut of DMX RGB lighting at Fluid Bar Exchange, a Berlin Fest nominee.

2018

First use of Bridgelux Dim-to-Warm (97CRI) at Ruia House. Expansion to Ahmedabad.

2019

HDFC Bank HQ and RGB lighting in Nasik executed. Footprint expands to Pune and Hyderabad.

2020

Studio opens. Launched 97CRI tunable white and anti-glare downlights. Executed key jewellery showrooms.

2021

Crossed 500 projects and 1M sq. ft. lit. Completed Marble Italia project (30K sq. ft.).

2022

Executed 100+ Bluestone stores. New Bhiwandi facility and high-CRI jewellery lighting launched.

2023

Released Play-100 downlights. Adopted 5S. Launched slim linear and 5mm magnetic lighting.

2024

Hit 1000+ projects. Expanded One Series. Debuted Concrete Lights and in-house ERP.

2025

Launch of Stone Series Gen 2, enhanced ERP, and new Concrete innovations.

FOUNDER'S DESK

"Lighting isn't just brightness—it's how we shape the feeling of a space."

Shashank Nayak founded Abner in 2015 to meet the growing demand for well-crafted architectural lighting in India. With a focus on technical precision and design integrity, his first breakthrough came via the JW Marriott, Juhu.

Today, Abner partners with brands like Orra, Bluestone, and Stone Ex, delivering adaptable, innovative lighting with an unwavering commitment to quality.
